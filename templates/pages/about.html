About Us


In this project, which was collaboratively designed by us , we learned about current trends in games and programming languages and created this project with the help of youtube tutorials. The products we created for this project were a website and reports for the college and university. We as a team used various web hosts to design their websites, the reports were designed in Ms. word and Powerpoint.


At the start of the project, we learned about several games and programming languages, learned about college requirements, met with and took help from both college friends and professors and learned more about the project. We then worked in groups of two to design our own project.
